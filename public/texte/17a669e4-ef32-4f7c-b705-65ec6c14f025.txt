<h1 class="post-title">Build a Microscope</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</header>
<div class="post-content">
<p>To build a standard microscope configuration, follow the assembly instructions below, which includes the download for the STL files.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3 id="openflexure-microscope-v7">OpenFlexure Microscope v7</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>v7 of the microscope is now in Beta. This means it&rsquo;s been tested by a small number of people, but we already believe it&rsquo;s much, much better than v6. We recommend you use v7 unless you have a particular reason to prefer v6.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a class="download-btn" href="https://build.openflexure.org/openflexure-microscope/v7.0.0-beta1/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Instructions and STL files for v7</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Before you start building, you may find it useful to look at the <a href="https://openflexure.org/projects/microscope/interactive-view-v7">interactive view of the microscope</a>.</p>
<p>Full release notes are available on <a href="https://gitlab.com/openflexure/openflexure-microscope/-/releases">GitLab</a>, but the headline features are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Rewritten instructions, with accurate bill of materials and rendered diagrams throughout.</li>
<li>Fully enclosed wiring.</li>
<li>No M3 screws threading into plastic, to eliminate stripped threads and damaged parts.</li>
<li>Improved illumination mounting, and PCB option to eliminate wire-to-wire soldering.</li>
<li>Standardised hardware: fewer types of screw required.</li>
<li>Better optics module mounting to get the objective at the right height.</li>
</ul>
<p>Under the hood, there has also been an extensive rewrite of the OpenSCAD code to make it more reliable and maintainable for the future. It&rsquo;s the best OpenFlexure Microscope yet!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3 id="openflexure-microscope-v6">OpenFlexure Microscope v6</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The previous version of the microscope is still accessible on the build server.<br>First, <a href="https://microscope-stls.openflexure.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">configure and download STLs</a>, then follow the <a href="https://build.openflexure.org/openflexure-microscope/v6.1.5/docs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">assembly instructions</a>. There is also an <a href="https://openflexure.org/projects/microscope/interactive-view-v6">interactive view of v6.1.5</a>.</p>
